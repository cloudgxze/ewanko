Target Partner Beneficiary

Kapitan Moy Elementary School

296A Dao St., West Drive, Marikina Heights, Marikina City

Objectives:

• Give value to real-life stories for young people based on books published by various Filipino writers brought to life in the Adarna House books.

• Improve children's listening and comprehension skills, which will be useful in the challenges ahead.

• Allow children to reflect and communicate with their peers and themselves more effectively.

• Can help in practicing and reminding people of good manners.
