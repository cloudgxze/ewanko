Description

A memo will be issued will contain all those event information (Date, Time, Objectives).

The team will think and do itinerary for the program to be made for the whole day. (Chosen Story, Roleplay, Q&A).

Materials:

Book - For selected stories that may come from library or to classmates who have copies of them.

Costume - For short plays of various parts of story.

Prizes - To encourage and entertain young participants in the Q&A portion. The class can do a donation drive for prizes.

Food - Water and simple snacks will be offered to the students of Kaptain Moy for the young attendees. Food will be purchased from funds to be raised from each member of the class. Some of the food are sandwiches and juices in tetra packs (Zesto).

Steps

1. The team will contact Kapitan Moy's Academic Officer Elementary School to communicate the program and goals.

2. Finalize how many and what stories will be used for the story telling. Then books of selected stories will be prepared.

3. Find or buy materials that will be needed for the dramatization.

4. Prepare for prizes and simple snacks like juice, tetrapack, & bread or biscuits or sandwiches.

5. Arrange equipment for the activity to be performed.
